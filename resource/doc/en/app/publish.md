# Publishing

Developers can upload their own copyrighted plugins to the app market, helping others while also benefiting from it.
Publishing address: https://www.workerman.net/app/create

Note: The **version number** filled in when publishing should match the version number in `plugin/{plugin}/config/app.php`.

## Listing Restrictions
**The following types of applications are not supported for listing:**

**Financial** Including but not limited to aggregate payment, rebates, lending, auctions, digital currency, blockchain  
**Board and card games** Including but not limited to lottery, treasure hunt, NiuNiu, prepaid cards, etc.  
**Data collection** Including but not limited to novels, comics, movies, TV shows, resource sharing, etc.  
**Overseas applications** Including but not limited to cloud hosting, VPN, etc.  
**Chat and customer service** Currently not supported for listing.
